A Short Story

A knight stands atop a hill.

He looks upon a valley of open opprotunity.

As he stands he sees a beast of enourmous size.

It towers over the trees growing beside it as it resides in the deepest part of the valley.

As the knight makes his way down the hill, he finds himself dreading the events that are about to happen.

A monster of enourmous size will now be the first to take down this valient knight.

It will be his end.

As he gets to the bottom of the hill he tredges through the deep valley.

He makes it to the clearing to find no beast.

He down at his feet to find the same beast only much smaller.

Copying his movement upon the ground.
